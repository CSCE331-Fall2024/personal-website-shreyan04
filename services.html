The Big Event

During The Big Event at Texas A&M University, I actively participated in a community service project focused on cleaning up roads and highways around the Bryan-College Station area. This annual event, which mobilizes thousands of students, provided me with the opportunity to contribute to the local community by improving the environment and ensuring safer, cleaner public spaces. My efforts, alongside those of my peers, helped to make a positive impact on the local infrastructure, reflecting my commitment to service and community engagement.

Red Cross - Houston

During Hurricane Beryl, I volunteered to distribute essential supplies to those affected by the storm. This involved organizing and handing out food, water, and other necessities to ensure that individuals and families had access to vital resources during the emergency. My efforts were part of a broader community response aimed at providing immediate relief and support to those in need, reflecting my commitment to helping others in times of crisis. This experience reinforced the importance of community solidarity and the impact of collective action in overcoming challenges.
